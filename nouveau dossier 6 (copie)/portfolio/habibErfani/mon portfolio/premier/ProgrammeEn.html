The computer science departments of the IUTs of France train students in computer science and teamwork in order to integrate them into professional life.

They offer a human-sized training, supervised by available teachers, as well as experienced professionals who provide students with hands-on practical experience in computer science.

Computer education (DUT or Professional License) is based on 3 axes, each of them requiring specific qualities and skills.

Analysis and design :

Preliminary work in relation with the client to specify his need and his expectations and the design of the schema of a computer processing.

Algorithmic and programming :

Effective implementation of processing until coding.

System architecture and networks :

Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...).
